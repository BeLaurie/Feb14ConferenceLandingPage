SourceRoad

Expanding pathways of collaboration, impact and empowerment through OpenSource for Black OpenSource maintainers and contributors.

What's SourceRoad?

On February 14th, 2017 in Honor of Black History Month join us for a special OpenSource Retreat where we will bring together Black* OpenSource maintainers, contributors and a few rising leaders. Join us for a day of fellowship, dialouge and connection. This event is FREE of charge to partipants (hotel and flight) and attendees will have the option to attend WONTFIX Cabal taking place the following day.

What's the story behind SourceRoad?

build. support. work together.

These are a few things that come to mind when we think about how Open source has enabled us to contribute to the technological solutions of our pesent time. As we continute to build solutons for the future how do we support ALL technologists and ensure their voices are heard and empowered.

Is there a fee to participate?

Nope! Selected participants will have flight and logdging provided.
